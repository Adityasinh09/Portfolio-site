About Me

Hi! I'm Aditya

A passionate UI/UX designer based in USA, with a Master’s degree in Computer Science from Troy University, focusing on Artificial Intelligence. I specialize in crafting intuitive user experiences and translating complex ideas into clean, accessible, and engaging digital interfaces.

With a strong foundation in design principles and front-end technologies, I’ve had the opportunity to design web applications, conduct user research, and build responsive and accessible designs during my roles at Thrive Way Outsourcing and Oasis Hospital Solution. Whether it's prototyping in Figma or bringing concepts to life with HTML, CSS, and JavaScript, I’m always driven to create meaningful user-centric solutions.

I’m especially passionate about design systems, branding, and accessibility. I believe great design is about more than just aesthetics—it’s about solving real problems and making digital experiences feel effortless and enjoyable.
